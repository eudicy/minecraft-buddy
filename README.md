# Minecraft Buddy

## Concept Summary

Ever started Minecraft and just... felt alone? No one to laugh with, ask "wait, is that thing gonna kill me?", or just hang out with while you explore. New players often feel lost, scared of dying, and honestly kind of bored playing solo — and a lot of them just quit before the game gets good.

**Target audience:** new Minecraft players, especially teens just getting started.

**The fix:** an AI buddy that plays right alongside you — reacting to what you're doing, answering your questions instantly, hyping you up or warning you before a creeper ruins your day. No more figuring it out alone. Minecraft becomes something you *share*, from your very first block.
